Our Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines how vulnerabilities.io, provided by Vulnerabilities Input Output Limited, ("we," "us," or "our"), a company registered in the UK with company number 14916604, collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use our services ("Services"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. Please read this Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we handle it.

Information We Collect​

1.1 Personal Information: We may collect personal information that you provide to us voluntarily when you interact with our Services. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, contact information, and any other information you choose to provide.

1.2 Usage Information: When you use our Services, we may automatically collect certain information about your usage patterns, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, and other technical data. We may also collect information about your interactions with our Services, including the pages you visit, the features you use, and the actions you take.

1.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience and collect information about how you use our Services. These technologies may collect information such as your browser type, preferences, and pages visited. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

Use of Personal Information​

2.1 Providing and Improving our Services: We use the personal information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our Services. This includes processing your inquiries, managing your account, delivering relevant content, and customizing your user experience.

2.2 Communication: We may use your personal information to communicate with you, such as responding to your inquiries, sending service-related notifications, and providing updates about our Services. We may also send you promotional and marketing communications, subject to your consent where required by applicable law.

2.3 Analytics and Aggregated Data: We may use the information we collect to analyse trends, monitor usage patterns, and gather statistical data to improve our Services. We may also aggregate and anonymize data for analytical purposes or to share with third parties for research, marketing, or other purposes, provided it does not personally identify you.

Data Sharing and Disclosure​

These service providers may have access to your personal information only to the extent necessary to perform their tasks and are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data. 3.2 Legal Compliance: We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., courts, government agencies). We may also disclose your information to enforce our rights, protect our property, or investigate and prevent illegal activities or suspected fraud.

3.3 Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity or parties involved as part of the transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice on our website if such a transfer occurs.
